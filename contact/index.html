About the author

Jeannie Lang loves writing for children and is delighted to publish her first book for them, being a passionate fan of their candid outlook on life, their creativity, and their intelligence. Her love of nature and her connection with young minds inspire her to create stories that she hopes will resonate with children everywhere. Her stories are about honouring their gifts, empowering them to be proud of who they are, and encouraging them to help shape the world around them.
